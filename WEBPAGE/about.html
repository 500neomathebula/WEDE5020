About Us

Safe Haven SA was founded in 2012 in Johannesburg by social workers and survivors of abuse. We provide shelter, counselling, legal aid, and empowerment programs.

MissionTo provide a safe, supportive environment for survivors of abuse and empower them to rebuild their lives.
VisionA South Africa free from gender-based violence, where every woman and child feels safe and valued.
